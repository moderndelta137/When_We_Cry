Features:

  • Unity 3D chess game.
  • Require two players.
  • Sophisticated Rule Design.

Instructions:

To play the demo:

Download the .rar and unzip.

  • Left_Click to Select.
  • Hold Mouse_Right_Button and drag to Rotate the board.
  • For detail rules, please read How_To_Play.txt

To view the source code:

Download the project folder and open with Unity.(Require Unity 5.2.2 or higher)
